🧠 What is a Brain-Computer Interface?

A Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) creates a direct communication pathway between the brain and external devices. It enables communication, movement restoration, and new forms of human-computer interaction.

How Does a BCI Work?

🧠
💻

Brain signals are detected, decoded using AI models, and translated into computer commands.
